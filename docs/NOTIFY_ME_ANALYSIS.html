8. Market Opportunities for a Competing App

Gap Analysis

Opportunity #1: Transparent Pricing

Build trust by offering clear, predictable pricing with no hidden fees, no transaction charges, and easy plan changes in both directions.

  • No percentage-based fees on pre-orders
  • Allow downgrading to any plan at any time
  • Price lock guarantee for existing customers

Opportunity #2: Easy Migration

Offer seamless migration tools to capture frustrated Notify Me! merchants locked into the platform.

  • CSV import with no record limits
  • API-based subscriber migration
  • Free migration assistance

Opportunity #3: Modular Features

Let merchants pay only for what they use instead of bundling everything together.

  • Back-in-stock only plan
  • Pre-order only plan
  • Wishlist as optional add-on

Opportunity #4: Technical Reliability

Focus on rock-solid fundamentals that Notify Me! struggles with.

  • Robust phone number validation (international formats)
  • Reliable email delivery with proper SPF/DKIM
  • Real-time inventory sync that actually works

Opportunity #5: Superior Support

Match or exceed competitor support standards.

  • 24/7 live chat (like STOQ)
  • Phone support for key markets (like Timesact)
  • Video onboarding calls
  • Never blame the merchant's systems

Do requests disappear when I hit the limit?

"Your requests remain intact regardless of subscription or notification limits."

"Thank you message notifications are NOT counted." However, SMS thank-you messages DO incur charges.

"No further notifications are sent out for subsequent restocks." Requests get 'Restocked' status after first notification.

"Notify Me! only tracks your Online Store sales channels." Other channels (POS, wholesale) are ignored for inventory.
